Welcome!

Thank you for visiting our site! This is research project associated with the Computational Methods for the Humanities course at the University of Pittsburgh. This site intends to document our research as our team explores gender portrayal across multiple editions of the classic Grimm's Fairytales. As our project develops, we anticipate the creation of new or more specific reseach questions. These are the questions that are currently facilitating our research:

Our research questions

  • Are the descriptors for women generally more positive or negative compared to men? Are women or men given a more active role?
  • How does the association between the fairy tales and marketing towards children lead to a shift in content within the stories, especially in regards to gender portrayal?

This site is under construction! Apologies if features are missing or work improperly.
